Happy Birthday, My Love

On this day the stars aligned,

A precious gift to all mankind.

The day the world became more bright,

When you arrived, my shining light.


Your smile outshines the morning sun,

Your laugh - my favorite song to hum.

Each year with you more precious grows,

Like petals on life's perfect rose.


So take my hand, my heart, my all,

As we dance through this birthday ball.

For you deserve the world and more,

My love for you forever pours.
